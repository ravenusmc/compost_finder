Connecting Gardeners with those that have compost

Basic Info

The goal of Compost Finder is to connect gardeners, who need trash for their compost bins, with people who want to donate their trash. Essentially, one person's trash is another person's gold

How it works

Compost Finder connects gardeners and people who want to turn over their organic trash together. It uses geolocation to only alert you to people in your immediate area who want to be part of the program

Facts about compost

Compost Information

Compost is using organic materials, that over a period of a few months, has turned into soil. Leaves, unused salad materials such as spinach, lettuce, etc, paper, cardboard are all good examples of compost material. Compost is how nature does it and you should to!

Why Compost

Compost is one of the best known ways to replenish your soil. Plants love it and you are not using chemicals to grow your plants! Also, if people were to compost more, it would help to cut down the amount of trash in land fills.
